## Manual

The Maude manual for Maude 3.5.1 is available in [PDF format](:maude/Maude3.5.1-manual.pdf) and in [HTML](https://maude.lcc.uma.es/maude-manual). The source code for the examples used in the manual is also available [here](:maude/Maude-3.5.1-manual-book-examples.zip) together with the examples from the Maude book.

## Book

The book [*All About Maude - A High-Performance Logical Framework*](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-540-71999-1) is published in Springer's series Lecture Notes on Computer Science 1234.

## Additional Resources

Some papers and reports on Maude, its formal foundations, and its applications are available in the [bibliography section](publications.md). A primer written for Maude 2.0.1 (but mostly applicable for later versions too) is available in [PDF](:maude/maude-primer.pdf) format. The examples are available as a tarred gzipped [archive](:maude/maude-primer-examples.tar.gz).
